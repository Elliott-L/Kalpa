About Kalpa

At the intersection of financial markets, technology, and talent.

Kalpa is a specialist search firm focused on research, trading, and investment technology. We work with firms where the quality of engineering, product, and technical leadership shapes research capability, execution performance, platform depth, and long-term edge.

Quietly positioned. Selectively engaged. Structured around mandates that require judgement, discretion, and real market reach.
