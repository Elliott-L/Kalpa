About Kalpa

At the intersection of trading, technology, and performance.

Kalpa is a specialist search firm focused on investment and trading technology. We work with firms where the quality of engineering, product, and technical leadership can shape execution, platform strength, research capability, and business performance.

Our focus is narrow by design. Deep specialisation creates better judgement around market structure, role calibration, technical context, and where exceptional capability actually sits.
