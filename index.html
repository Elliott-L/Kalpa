Built to connect investment and trading technology firms with the talent that drives performance.

Markets

Proprietary Trading & Market Making
High-frequency, low-latency trading systems, market data pipelines and execution infrastructure where microseconds matter — from kernel bypass networking and FPGA optimisation to order routing across traditional and digital markets.
Systematic & Quantitative Hedge Funds
Quant research and production systems: signal generation, data pipelines, backtesting frameworks, high-performance compute and modern research workflows incorporating machine learning and LLM tooling.
Multi-Manager Platforms
Multi-strategy, pod-based platforms built on shared data, risk and execution systems. Unified infrastructure supporting independent teams developing and deploying strategies across asset classes — increasingly built around AI-driven workflows and real-time data layers.
Investment Banks
Front-office quant, electronic trading and technology functions at tier-1 investment banks. Modular execution platforms, API-driven trading architecture, algorithmic systems and the principal flow infrastructure underpinning modern dealer businesses.
Asset Managers
Quantitative and systematic asset managers investing heavily in technology transformation — from AI-driven research workflows and cloud data infrastructure to platform modernisation and the ML and quant engineering talent building the next generation of investment technology at institutional scale.
Trading Technology & Market Infrastructure
OMS, EMS, risk platforms, market data infrastructure, alternative data, AI and analytics tooling, compute and hardware — and the exchanges, trading venues and market infrastructure providers that the rest of the industry depends on.
