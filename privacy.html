6. Our lawful bases for processing

Depending on the context, we rely on one or more of the following lawful bases under UK data protection law:

  • Legitimate interests — where processing is necessary for our legitimate interests or those of a client, and those interests are not overridden by your rights and interests.
  • Consent — where we ask for and rely on your consent for a specific use.
  • Contract — where processing is necessary to take steps at your request or to perform a contract with you.
  • Legal obligation — where we need to process personal data to comply with applicable law or regulation.
